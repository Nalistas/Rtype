{\bfseries{All bug reports and problem issues require a js\+Fiddle}}. ~\newline
 \href{http://jsfiddle.net/simeydotme/fmo50w7n/}{\texttt{ Please fork this JSFiddle as a baseline}}.


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item A test case clearly demonstrates the bug or issue.
\item It contains the bare minimum HTML, CSS, and Java\+Script required to demonstrate the bug.
\item Assets are not minified (we cannot debug .min.\+js files).
\item A link to your production site is {\bfseries{not}} a reduced test case.
\end{DoxyItemize}

Providing a test case is the best way to get your issue addressed. Without a test case; your issue may be closed. ~\newline
 You must provide a clear and simple way to reproduce the issue with the provided fiddle.\hypertarget{md__2home_2solype_2delivery_2current__days_2trello_2front_2node__modules_2slick-carousel_2CONTRIBUTING_autotoc_md12302}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Support}{Support}}\label{md__2home_2solype_2delivery_2current__days_2trello_2front_2node__modules_2slick-carousel_2CONTRIBUTING_autotoc_md12302}
